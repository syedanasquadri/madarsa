About Al-Nur Islamic School

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide an excellent academic education rooted in Islamic values, fostering intellectual growth, spiritual development, and strong moral character in our students. We envision a community of lifelong learners and responsible citizens who contribute positively to society, guided by the teachings of the Quran and Sunnah.

Our Values

  • Knowledge (Ilm): Emphasizing both secular and religious learning.
  • Integrity (Ikhlas): Promoting honesty and sincerity in all actions.
  • Compassion (Rahmah): Nurturing empathy and kindness towards others.
  • Excellence (Itqan): Striving for mastery and high standards in every endeavor.
  • Community (Ummah): Building a supportive and inclusive school family.

Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to creating a safe, nurturing, and intellectually stimulating environment where every child can thrive.

Academics & Programs

Islamic Studies

Our comprehensive Islamic Studies program includes Quranic recitation (Tajweed), memorization (Hifz), Tafsir, Hadith, Fiqh, and Arabic language. We aim to instill a profound understanding and love for Islam.

General Studies

We follow a rigorous national curriculum for subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English Language Arts, Social Studies, and Computer Science, preparing students for higher education and future careers.

Grade Levels

Al-Nur Islamic School proudly serves students from Pre-Kindergarten to Grade 12, offering a continuous and progressive learning journey adapted to each developmental stage.

Admissions

How to Apply

  1. **Inquiry:** Submit an online inquiry form or call our admissions office.
  2. **Visit:** Schedule a school tour and attend an information session.
  3. **Application:** Complete the application form and submit required documents.
  4. **Assessment:** Students undergo a placement assessment.
  5. **Interview:** Parent/guardian and student interview with school administration.
  6. **Decision:** Notification of admission decision.

Requirements

Birth Certificate, Previous School Records, Immunization Records, Passport/ID Copies, Application Fee.
